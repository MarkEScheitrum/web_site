How to Use the Genealogy Recorder

  1. Click this Genealogy Recorder link to open the recorder page.
  2. In the recorder, click the "Load Records" Action Button.
  3. Enter the Passphrase from the email. It is case sensitive.
  4. Edit records with this guidance:
    • View Table to see your records (extracted from the Leiby Genealogy Book, 2016 edition).
    • Use the action buttons to add spouses/partners, children, or record deaths as needed.
    • Use the "Update Record" button to fix typos or omissions in editable fields.
  5. Review the resulting changes in the table.
  6. If satisfied, click the "Return Updates" button.
  7. Feedback welcome! Use the Report an Issue form to share issues or suggest improvements.
